Our Perfume Women

Aureva - Lune

LUNE reflects the beauty of quiet confidence. A graceful composition of luminous florals, soft fruits, creamy vanilla, and elegant woods creates a scent that feels warm, comforting, and effortlessly refined. Delicate yet enduring, LUNE is made for women who embrace elegance in its purest form.


 

TOP NOTES : Cassis

MIDDLE NOTES : May Rose, Freesia

BASE NOTES : Vanilla, Patchouli, Woody Notes


 

WHEN TO WEAR : Winter, Fall, Day, Night

Aureva - Remember

REMEMBER captures the beauty of moments worth holding onto. A luminous blend of delicate florals, crisp fruits, soft musk, and airy freshness unfolds into a scent that feels clean, comforting, and effortlessly elegant. Designed to become your everyday signature, it leaves a subtle yet unforgettable impression wherever life takes you.


 

TOP NOTES : Pear, Melon

MIDDLE NOTES : Freesia, Rose

BASE NOTES : Musk, Patchouli, Amber


 

WHEN TO WEAR : Spring, Summer, Day

Aureva - Homme

HOMME is an expression of effortless confidence. Bright citrus and warm cinnamon ignite the opening before settling into a refined heart of amber, leather, and woods. Sophisticated yet approachable, powerful yet balanced, it is a signature scent crafted for the modern gentleman who values presence over attention.


 

TOP NOTES : Blood Mandarin, Grapefruit, Mint

MIDDLE NOTES : Cinnamon, Spicy Notes, Rose

BASE NOTES : Amber, Leather, Woody Notes, Indian Patchouli


 

WHEN TO WEAR : Winter, Fall, Night

Aureva - Eclipse

ECLIPSE is where warmth meets mystery. A sophisticated composition of creamy vanilla, warm spices, smooth tobacco, and subtle woody nuances creates a luxurious scent that evolves beautifully throughout the day. Rich yet balanced, bold yet refined crafted for those who embrace confidence with effortless elegance.


 

TOP NOTES : Tobacco Leaf, Spicy Notes

MIDDLE NOTES : Vanilla, Cacao, Tonka Bean, Tobacco Blossom

BASE NOTES : Dried Fruit, Woody Notes


 

WHEN TO WEAR : Winter, Fall, Night
